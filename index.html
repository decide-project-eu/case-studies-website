How the cattle barometer was transformed to fit multiple labs across Europe in the DECIDE project!

Within WP1 and WP3 we have been working to transform an existing tool called the Griepbarometer developed in Flanders, Belgium into a international tool applicable across Europe, fit to several diagnostic laboratories.

Welcome to the website of the Decide Project use cases, a Horizon 2020 project from the European Commission. This site and its content are being created and curated by the work package leaders and data leaders.
